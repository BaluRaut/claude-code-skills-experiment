# DOC-9 · Filter appointments by doctor & date

Type: Story · Points: 3 · Priority: Low (stretch)

## Description
As a patient with many appointments, I want to filter the list.

## Acceptance criteria
- AC-1 I can filter the appointments list by doctor.
- AC-2 I can filter by a specific date.
- AC-3 Filters combine (doctor AND date) and can be cleared.
- AC-4 An active filter with no matches shows an empty state distinct from
  "no appointments at all".

## Out of scope
- Saved filters, URL-persisted filters.
